Tic Tac Chess

Tic Tac Chess is a two-player strategy game that combines the logic of Tic Tac Toe with the movement mechanics of chess. Each player controls chess pieces, aiming to align their three chosen pieces in a row to win. Unlike traditional chess, no pieces can be captured, so every move requires careful planning and foresight. I developed the game in C# (Windows Forms), handling turn-based logic, valid chess movement rules, and win detection. This project deepened my skills in object-oriented programming, game logic design, and integrating hardware with software.

Tic Tac Chess

A Strategic Fusion of Two Classic Games

How It Works

🎯 Objective

Align three of your chosen chess pieces in a row—horizontally, vertically, or diagonally—to win the game.

♟️ Chess Movement

Each piece moves according to traditional chess rules: knights jump in L-shapes, bishops move diagonally, rooks move in straight lines, etc.

🛡️ No Captures

Unlike traditional chess, pieces cannot capture each other. All pieces remain on the board, requiring careful spatial planning.

🔄 Turn-Based

Players alternate turns, moving one piece at a time. Every decision counts as the board fills up with strategic possibilities.

Game Interface

The main game interface shows the 3x3 board where players place their chess pieces. Each player selects their pieces at the start, and the interface highlights valid moves when a piece is selected. The clean design makes it easy to see the board state at a glance.

Piece Selection

Before the game starts, each player chooses three chess pieces from their available options. the players can also choose to use items. these can be used to help yourself win or keep the enemy from winning. this adds a new layer of dificulty and planning to the game.

Arduino Robot Arm Integration

The game connects to a physical robot arm that moves real chess pieces on an actual board. When a move is made in the software, the Arduino receives coordinates and precisely picks up the piece, moves it to the new position, and places it down. This bridges the digital game with a physical experience.

Win Detection System

After each move, the game checks all possible winning combinations. When three pieces align in a row, the winning player is announced and the game ends and can be played all over again.

GroundCrashers

A turn-based strategy toys-to-life game

How It Works

🎮 RFID Creatures

Scan physical creature card with RFID technology to bring them into the game. Each figurine stores its stats and abilities.

⚔️ Turn-Based Combat

Strategically choose attacks and abilities each turn. Plan your moves carefully to defeat opponents in tactical battles.

📖 Story Mode

Progress through an engaging storyline, facing increasingly challenging battles and unlocking new creatures along the way.

🔄 Collectible System

Build your collection of creatures, each with unique stats, abilities, and battle strategies to master.

Main Menu Interface

The main menu provides access to story mode and versus battles. The interface is designed to be intuitive and engaging, with clear navigation to the game features. Players can quickly jump into battles or go into the story game.

RFID Creature Scanning

Place your physical creature card on the RFID reader to scan it into the game. The system reads the unique ID from each figurine and loads all your creatures in a tab where you can chose which ones you want to use. This bridges the physical toy collection with the digital game world.

Battle System

The turn-based battle system displays both creatures with their health bars, stats, and available moves. Players choose from different attacks and special abilities each turn, creating strategic gameplay where type advantages and move selection matter. Visual feedback shows damage dealt and status effects. the creatures stats react to the biome, weather and time of day so keep that in mind while chosing creatures

Story Map

Navigate through multiple diverse world maps featuring multiple biomes, each with its unique visual style and challenges. As you progress through the map, battles become increasingly difficult, requiring you to strategize and strengthen your creature team. Each biome introduces new enemy types and tougher GroundCrashers protecting the chaos orbs.

Story Campaign

Follow the journey of two heroes fighting against GroundCrashers who are protecting chaos orbs scattered across the world. Your mission is to find and encage these powerful orbs to save the world from chaos.

Quizmester

A competitive quiz application with multiple game modes

How It Works

📝 Multiple Categories

Choose from various quiz categories including sports, science, history, and more. Each category has 20+ unique questions to test your knowledge.

⏱️ Three Game Modes

Normal mode with a countdown timer, Sudden Death where one wrong answer ends the game, and Infinite Time for a relaxed experience.

🏆 Leaderboard System

Compete for the top 10 high scores. Your score is calculated from correct answers plus bonus points for remaining time.

🎮 Custom Quizzes

Create your own quizzes with custom questions and answers. Share them with other players to test their knowledge.

Login & Registration

Users can create an account with a unique username and password or log in with existing credentials. The system securely stores user information in a database. Each user's game history and scores are tracked separately, allowing for personalized leaderboards and progress tracking.

Quiz Overview

The quiz selection screen displays the quiz name, creator, and the top 10 high scores for that specific quiz. Players can choose between three game modes: Normal (timed quiz), Sudden Death (one mistake and you're out), or Infinite Time (no time pressure). This screen gives players all the information they need before starting a quiz.

Quiz Gameplay

During gameplay, questions are displayed with four multiple-choice answers that appear in random order each time. A countdown timer keeps track of remaining time, and players can skip one question if needed. The interface shows the current score and question number, providing clear feedback throughout the quiz. Each correct answer adds points to your total score.

Score Breakdown

After completing a quiz, the score screen provides a detailed breakdown showing the quiz name, creator, answer score (points from correct answers), time score (bonus points for remaining time), and total score. If your score makes the top 10, you'll see your ranking on the leaderboard. This comprehensive view helps players understand their performance and strive for improvement.

Quiz Creator

An additional feature I added beyond the assignment requirements: players can create their own custom quizzes. The quiz creator interface allows users to add questions, define four answer options, mark the correct answer, and assign categories. This feature encourages community engagement and provides endless content for players to enjoy and share with others.

Admin Panel

The administrator panel provides full CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) functionality for managing users and questions. Admins can add new questions to any category, edit existing questions, remove inappropriate content, and manage user accounts. This ensures quiz quality remains high and users have a safe, enjoyable experience. The admin interface is intuitive and powerful, making content management straightforward.
